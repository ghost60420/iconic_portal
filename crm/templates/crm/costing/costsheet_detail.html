How to use this costing sheet
  1. Enter order quantity.
  2. Enter fabric cost per piece and wastage percent.
  3. Enter materials and trims (rib, zipper, labels, accessories, etc.).
  4. Enter sewing, overhead, process, packaging, freight, testing, and other costs.
  5. Check total cost per piece and total order cost.
  6. Enter quote price per piece to see margin.
  7. Export PDF or Excel.
  8. Files will appear inside the Opportunity.
Tips: Always include fabric wastage - Do not forget packaging and freight - Use actual production data later to compare.
