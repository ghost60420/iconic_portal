Costing Input Guide
i
Rules to follow
  1. Most fields are COST PER PIECE. Enter the cost for one garment only.
  2. If a supplier gives price per kg or per meter, convert to per piece before entering. Example: price 5 per kg, consumption 0.30 kg, enter 1.50 per piece.
  3. Wastage is PERCENT only. Example: 3 means 3 percent.
  4. Some costs are PER ORDER only: freight, testing, compliance, courier. Enter the total order cost. The system will divide by order quantity.
  5. Sewing charge is always PER PIECE. Enter labor cost for one garment.
  6. Before quoting a customer, confirm: Total cost per piece, quote price per piece, profit per piece, margin percent.
  7. Never enter supplier unit price directly like 5 per kg. Always convert to per piece.
Quick check
Total cost per piece must be lower than quote price per piece. If not, it is a loss.
