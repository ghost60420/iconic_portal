How to input costing without mistakes

  1. Open an Opportunity and click Create costing.
  2. Fill the Header section (quantity, factory, currency, exchange rate).
  3. Add line items under Fabric, Sewing trims, Packaging trims, and Other.
  4. If you have labor SMV, fill the SMV section.
  5. Save, then review Summary & Pricing.
  6. Approve when all values are correct.

Input rules

  • Most prices are BDT per piece.
  • If supplier price is per kg/meter/roll/cone, use consumption and denominator to convert.
  • Wastage is percent only, not money.
  • Per order costs are divided by order quantity automatically.
  • Confirm total cost per piece before quoting.
